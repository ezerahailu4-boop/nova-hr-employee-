Nova HR SM

Nova HR Supply & Management PLC

Strategic Human Resource Management for Sustainable Growth

Nova HR SM is Ethiopia's trusted HR consulting and workforce solutions company, headquartered in Addis Ababa. We partner with businesses of all sizes to source top talent, manage HR operations, ensure legal compliance, and build high-performing teams across all industries — from finance and hospitality to construction and manufacturing.

🎯

Right Talent, Right Fit

We don't just fill positions — we find candidates who align with your culture, values, and long-term goals.

⚖️

100% Legal Compliance

Full adherence to Ethiopia's Labor Proclamation No. 1156/2019 and ERCA tax regulations.

⚡

Fast & Reliable

Rapid turnaround on placements with a rigorous screening process to ensure quality every time.

🤝

Long-Term Partnership

We build lasting relationships with both employers and candidates — transparent, fair, and professional.

What We Do

🎯
Talent Sourcing & Recruitment

End-to-end recruitment for permanent, contract, and temporary positions. We source, screen, interview, and place candidates across all sectors in Ethiopia.

🏢
HR Outsourcing & Workforce Management

We manage your entire HR function — employee records, contracts, disciplinary procedures, and day-to-day HR administration — so you can focus on your core business.

🎓
Training & Development

Customized learning and development programs for all levels — from frontline staff to senior management. We design, deliver, and measure training effectiveness.

💰
Payroll Management

Accurate, timely, and fully compliant payroll processing including income tax (PAYE), pension contributions, and all statutory deductions per ERCA requirements.

🔒
Manpower Supply

Deployment of trained security guards, cleaners, drivers, receptionists, logistics staff, and general laborers to client sites across Addis Ababa and beyond.

📊
Performance Management

Design and implementation of KPI frameworks, performance appraisal systems, and employee evaluation tools that drive accountability and results.

⚖️
HR Consulting & Legal Advisory

Expert guidance on Ethiopian labor law, employment contracts, disciplinary hearings, termination procedures, and HR policy development.
